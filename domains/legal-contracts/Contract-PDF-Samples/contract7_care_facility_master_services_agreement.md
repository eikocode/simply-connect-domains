## 1. SERVICES AND SCOPE

1.1 Provider shall supply, install, and support the Memora Smart Companion System for Customer's elderly care facilities, as detailed in **Exhibit A (Statement of Work)**.

1.2 **System Components:**
- **Smart Companion (Model MC-110):** Edge interaction unit with optical sensor, speaker, display, and actuatable element for conversation + animation.
- **Link Device (Model LD-115):** Secure primary datastore hub with **no input/output elements**, designed for proximity-based synchronization via Personal Area Network (PAN).
- **Software Services:** Interaction engine, persona modeling, spaced repetition algorithm, hybrid storage management, and secure cloud backup.

1.3 **Deployment Scope:** Initial deployment of **fifty (50) units** across Customer's Hong Kong facilities, with option to expand to two hundred (200) units upon mutual agreement.

## 3. DATA HANDLING AND PRIVACY

3.1 **Hybrid Storage Architecture:**
- **Local Storage:** User interaction data is temporarily stored on Smart Companion (MC-110) for real-time functionality (maximum 24 hours).
- **Secure Sync:** When Link Device (LD-115) is within proximity (≤3 meters), data is encrypted (AES-256) and transferred to primary datastore.
- **Automatic Erasure:** Upon successful synchronization confirmation, data is automatically erased from Smart Companion within 5 seconds.

3.2 **Customer Responsibilities:**
- Ensure Link Devices are charged and within PAN range of Smart Companions at least once daily to prevent data loss.
- Obtain explicit consent from end users (or legal guardians) for biometric data collection (facial recognition, optical position tracking).
- Comply with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, PDPO, HIPAA if applicable).

3.3 **Provider Responsibilities:**
- Implement encryption for data in transit and at rest.
- Provide audit logs for synchronization events and erasure confirmations.
- Notify Customer of data breaches within 24 hours of discovery.

3.4 **Data Processing Agreement:** The parties shall execute a separate Data Processing Agreement (DPA) compliant with GDPR/PDPO as **Exhibit B**, governing processing of personal data.
