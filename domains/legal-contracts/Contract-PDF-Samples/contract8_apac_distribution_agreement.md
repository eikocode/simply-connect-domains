## Exhibit B: End-User Privacy Terms Template

*[Distributor shall incorporate this language into end-user terms for Products sold in Territory]*

**Data Storage and Erasure**
Your interaction data is temporarily stored on the Smart Companion (Model MC-110) for real-time functionality (maximum 24 hours). When your Link Device (Model LD-115) is within proximity (≤3 meters), data is encrypted and transferred to the secure primary datastore on the Link Device. Upon successful synchronization, data is automatically erased from the Smart Companion within 5 seconds. Please sync your devices regularly to avoid data loss.

**Biometric Data Consent**
By using this Product, you explicitly consent to the collection and processing of biometric data (facial recognition, optical position tracking) for: (1) authenticating your identity; (2) adjusting animation and tone based on your position and mood; (3) improving personalization. You may withdraw consent at any time via Settings > Privacy, which may limit Product functionality.

**Your Rights**
You have the right to access, correct, or request erasure of your personal data. Contact privacy@memora.tech to exercise these rights. We respond within 30 days.

**Data Sharing**
We do not sell your personal data. Aggregated, anonymized insights may be shared with research partners to improve cognitive support tools. You may opt out of research sharing via Settings.

## Exhibit C: Product Specifications (Reference)

**Smart Companion (Model MC-110)**
- Dimensions: 180mm x 120mm x 80mm
- Weight: 650g
- Input: 4-channel microphone array, capacitive touch, optical sensor (OmniVision OV9282)
- Output: 2W speaker, 3.5" e-ink display, actuatable robotic head (Maxon EC-i 40)
- Storage: 32GB eMMC (peripheral datastore), AES-256 encrypted
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth 5.2 for PAN sync
- Power: 5V/2A USB-C charging; battery life 8 hours active use
- Operating Temp: 0°C to 45°C

**Link Device (Model LD-115)**
- Dimensions: 100mm x 100mm x 30mm
- Weight: 250g
- Storage: 256GB NVMe SSD (primary datastore), hardware encryption (Microchip ATECC608B)
- Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.2 / IEEE 802.15.4 PAN transceiver (Nordic nRF52840)
- **Critical: No I/O elements** – no screen, buttons, ports, speakers, or microphones
- Security: Tamper-evident seals; access via authenticated MC-110 only
- Power: Wireless charging (Qi standard); battery life 30 days standby
- Operating Temp: 0°C to 45°C

**Software Requirements**
- Interaction Engine: Adaptive spaced repetition (DSR model), persona modeling, multimodal sync
- Sync Protocol: PAN-triggered (RSSI ≤-70dBm for ≤3m), TLS 1.3 encryption, ECDSA P-256 authentication
- Erasure: Automatic within 5 seconds of sync confirmation; cryptographic shredding (NIST 800-88)
