# ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

**Date:** January 14, 2026  
**Assignor:** Precision Components Ltd. ("Supplier")  
**Assignee:** Memora Technologies Limited ("Buyer")  
**Related Agreement:** Hardware Supplier Agreement No. HSA-2026-001-HK  

---

## 1. Consideration
For good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are acknowledged (including access to proprietary specifications and payment under the Related Agreement), Assignor hereby assigns to Assignee all right, title, and interest in the Work Product defined below.

## 2. Definition of Work Product
"Work Product" means any inventions, designs, improvements, discoveries, or works of authorship created by Assignor in connection with the Related Agreement, specifically including:
1.  **Link Device Designs:** Any modifications to the Model LD-115 enclosure that eliminate user-accessible I/O elements (ports, buttons, screens) for security purposes.
2.  **Synchronization Logic:** Any firmware improvements related to PAN-triggered data synchronization or proximity-based handshake protocols.
3.  **Manufacturing Processes:** Any specialized manufacturing processes developed specifically for Buyer's secure storage architecture (e.g., tamper-evident seal application).
4.  **Derivatives:** Any derivatives or adaptations of the above.

## 3. Assignment
Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns to Assignee:
- All global patent rights, copyright, trade secret rights, and moral rights (to the extent waivable) in the Work Product.
- The right to file, prosecute, and enforce patent applications related to the Work Product.
- All future rights and extensions thereof.

## 4. Assistance
Assignor agrees to cooperate with Assignee, at Assignee's expense, to:
- Execute further documents necessary to perfect the assignment.
- Assist in patent prosecution (e.g., signing inventor declarations).
- Provide records or disclosures related to the Work Product.

## 5. No Retained Rights
Assignor retains no rights to use, license, or sell the Work Product except as expressly licensed back in the Related Agreement (if any).

## 6. Governing Law
This Assignment shall be governed by the laws of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region.
