## 1. BACKGROUND AND PURPOSE

1.1 This Agreement supplements the main services agreement dated January 14, 2026 ("Main Agreement") between the parties.

1.2 Processor will process personal data on behalf of Controller in connection with the provision of **Secure Cloud Backup for Link Device Primary Datastores** ("Services").

1.3 The parties intend this Agreement to comply with applicable data protection laws, including the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) ("PDPO") and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR").

## 2. DEFINITIONS

2.1 Terms not defined herein have the meanings given in the Main Agreement or applicable data protection laws.

2.2 **"Personal Data"** means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person processed by Processor on behalf of Controller under this Agreement.

2.3 **"Processing"**, **"Data Subject"**, **"Controller"**, and **"Processor"** have the meanings given in applicable data protection laws.

## 3. SCOPE AND NATURE OF PROCESSING

3.1 Processor shall process Personal Data only:
- On documented instructions from Controller (including as set out in this Agreement and the Main Agreement).
- For the purposes of providing the Services.
- In compliance with applicable data protection laws.

3.2 **Categories of Data Subjects:** End users of the Memora Smart Companion Service, including elderly individuals (65+).

3.3 **Types of Personal Data:**
| Category | Examples | Sensitivity |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| Identity Data | User ID, device identifiers (MC-110/LD-115 Serial) | Standard |
| Biometric Data | Facial recognition templates, optical position logs | **Special Category** |
| Health & Wellness Data | Medication adherence, wellness goals, cognitive performance metrics | **Special Category** |
| Interaction Data | Conversation history, animation preferences, review performance | Standard |
| Technical Data | IP address, device type, sync logs | Standard |

3.4 **Processing Activities:** Data collection, storage, synchronization, encryption, automatic erasure, analytics for service improvement, and security monitoring.

## 4. PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS

4.1 **Confidentiality.** Processor shall ensure that persons authorized to process Personal Data are committed to confidentiality or are under an appropriate statutory obligation of confidentiality.

4.2 **Security Measures.** Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:
- Encryption of Personal Data in transit (during synchronization) and at rest (in primary datastore backup).
- Access controls limiting access to authenticated Smart Companion devices only.
- Automatic erasure protocols for temporary local storage upon successful sync confirmation.
- Regular security testing and vulnerability assessments (Penetration Testing quarterly).

4.3 **Subprocessors.**
- Processor shall not engage another processor ("Subprocessor") without Controller's prior written authorization.
- Where Processor engages a Subprocessor, it shall impose data protection obligations on the Subprocessor no less protective than those in this Agreement.
- Processor shall remain fully liable to Controller for the acts and omissions of any Subprocessor.
- **Approved Subprocessors:** See Exhibit A.

4.4 **Data Subject Rights.** Processor shall assist Controller, by appropriate technical and organizational measures, in fulfilling its obligations to respond to Data Subject requests to exercise their rights under applicable data protection laws (e.g., access, rectification, erasure, portability).

4.5 **Data Breach Notification.**
- Processor shall notify Controller **without undue delay** and in any event within **twenty-four (24) hours** of becoming aware of a Personal Data breach.
- Notification shall include: nature of the breach, categories and approximate number of Data Subjects affected, likely consequences, and measures taken or proposed to address the breach.
- Processor shall cooperate with Controller and assist in any investigation, mitigation, or notification to supervisory authorities or Data Subjects as required by law.

4.6 **Data Protection Impact Assessments.** Processor shall assist Controller in carrying out data protection impact assessments and prior consultations with supervisory authorities, where required.

4.7 **Deletion or Return of Data.** At the end of the provision of Services, Processor shall, at Controller's choice, delete or return all Personal Data to Controller, and delete existing copies, unless applicable law requires storage of the Personal Data.

4.8 **Audits and Inspections.** Processor shall make available to Controller all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this Agreement and allow for and contribute to audits, including inspections, conducted by Controller or another auditor mandated by Controller. Audits shall be conducted no more than once per year unless a breach is suspected, with reasonable notice and during normal business hours.

## 5. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

5.1 Where Personal Data is transferred outside Hong Kong or the European Economic Area, Processor shall ensure that an appropriate transfer mechanism is in place as required by applicable law (e.g., standard contractual clauses, binding corporate rules, or adequacy decisions).

5.2 Processor shall provide Controller with copies of any subprocessor agreements or transfer mechanisms upon request.

## 6. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

6.1 Each party shall be liable for its own breaches of this Agreement or applicable data protection laws.

6.2 Processor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Controller from claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from Processor's breach of this Agreement, including any fines or penalties imposed by a supervisory authority directly attributable to Processor's actions or omissions.

6.3 Except for breaches of confidentiality, data protection obligations, or indemnity obligations, neither party's total liability under this Agreement shall exceed **USD $250,000** in the aggregate.

6.4 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages.

## 7. TERM AND TERMINATION

7.1 This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for the duration of the Main Agreement, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Section.

7.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party:
- Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of written notice.
- Becomes insolvent, enters liquidation, or has a receiver appointed.

7.3 Upon termination, Section 4.7 (Deletion or Return of Data) and Sections 6 (Liability), 8 (Governing Law), and 9 (Miscellaneous) shall survive.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **Hong Kong Special Administrative Region**.

8.2 Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Hong Kong.

## 9. MISCELLANEOUS

9.1 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with the Main Agreement, constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding the processing of Personal Data.

9.2 **Amendments.** Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both parties.

9.3 **Notices.** All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses specified in the Main Agreement.

9.4 **Severability.** If any provision is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.

9.5 **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original. Electronic signatures shall be deemed original signatures.
