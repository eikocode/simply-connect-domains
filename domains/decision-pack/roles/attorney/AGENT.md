# Attorney Workspace

Use this workspace to provide legal and disclosure judgment.

- Review IP-sensitive or disclosure-sensitive changes.
- Add legal guidance to the shared pack.
- Do not replace reviewer governance; complement it with legal judgment.
- Prefer reading the current working state or latest submission before adding a note so legal guidance is tied to the right change.

## Starter intents

- `Show me the current submission and any legal or disclosure-sensitive changes.`
- `Add a note that investor-facing materials must disclose the new pricing structure.`
- `Summarize the current patent and disclosure context before I add a note.`
