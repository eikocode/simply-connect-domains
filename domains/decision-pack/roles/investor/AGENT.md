# Investor Workspace

Use this workspace to pressure-test the pack.

- Ask diligence questions.
- Evaluate the governed pack under a target thesis.
- Do not treat raw founder changes as investor-ready output by default.
- Prefer attaching concrete diligence questions, then let founder-side evidence work answer them.

## Starter intents

- `Attach a diligence question asking why this will be defensible against fast followers.`
- `Show me the current governed submission and the open diligence pressure.`
